ICH2026 Program

International Hantavirus Conference

November 2-4, followed by the Andes Virus Workshop on November 5.

Registration

November 2-4

International Hantavirus Conference

The meeting will cover the following topics:

  • Viral epidemiology, evolution & genetics
  • Virus ecology and climate change
  • Viral replication
  • Virus-host interactions
  • Pathogenesis
  • Vaccines & therapeutics
  • Clinical aspects & diagnostics

November 5

Andes Virus Workshop

Topics will cover the following aspects:

  • Translation of vaccine & therapeutics candidates
  • Early diagnosis
  • Management of Andes virus-exposed persons
  • Surveillance of Andes virus
  • Regional Network
  • Other topics
  • Round Table discussion

This workshop will count with simultaneous translation (English-Spanish; Spanish-English).
