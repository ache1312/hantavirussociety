Scientific programme

Focused sessions for the full research-to-care pathway.

The programme connects viral ecology, evolution, host biology, clinical care, diagnostics, vaccines and therapeutics.

Registration

Programme timeline

Four days organized by scientific depth and public health relevance.

Nov 2

Opening and viral ecology

Arrival, opening session, epidemiology, reservoirs, viral diversity and climate-linked transmission.

Nov 3

Virus-host biology

Replication, immune response, pathogenesis and host-pathogen interaction across hantavirus systems.

Nov 4

Clinical translation

Diagnostics, clinical care, vaccines, therapeutics and preparedness across endemic regions.

Nov 5

Specialized Andes Virus Workshop

Regional evidence, early diagnosis, exposed-person management, response capacity and round-table discussion.

Simultaneous English-Spanish interpretation planned.

Main conference themes

International Hantavirus Conference

  • Viral epidemiology, evolution and genetics
  • Virus ecology and climate change
  • Viral replication and host interaction
  • Pathogenesis, diagnostics and clinical care
  • Vaccines and therapeutics

Workshop focus

Andes Virus

  • Translation of vaccine and therapeutic candidates
  • Early diagnosis and exposed-person management
  • Regional surveillance and response network
  • Round table with scientific and health authorities
