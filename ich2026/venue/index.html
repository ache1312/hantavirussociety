ICH2026 Venue & Travel

Hotel Bellavista, Puerto Varas.

The 2026 meeting will take place in Hotel Bellavista, Puerto Varas.

Open mapHotel Bellavista

How to get to Puerto Varas

International arrival, domestic flight and local transfer.

01International arrival - Santiago (SCL)
02Domestic flight - Santiago to Puerto Montt (PMC)
03Transfer - Puerto Montt Airport to Puerto Varas

Step 1

International Arrival - Santiago (SCL)

Most international travelers will arrive at Arturo Merino Benitez International Airport (SCL) in Santiago, Chile.

  • Proceed through immigration and customs
  • Collect your luggage
  • Follow signs for domestic connections

Tip: Allow at least 2-3 hours for connection time, especially if arriving from long-haul international flights.

Alternatively, you may prefer to make a stop-over in Santiago. There are several hotels near the airport. Also, it is possible to book day trips to diverse destinations, close to, or in Santiago.

Step 2

Domestic Flight - Santiago to Puerto Montt (PMC)

From Santiago, take a domestic flight to El Tepual Airport (PMC) in Puerto Montt.

  • Flight duration: approximately 1 hour 40 minutes
  • Airlines operate multiple daily flights

Tip: Puerto Montt is the main gateway to the Los Lagos region and Puerto Varas.

Step 3

Transfer - Puerto Montt Airport to Puerto Varas

Puerto Varas is located approximately 20-30 km from the airport, with a travel time of about 25-30 minutes depending on traffic.

Private or Shared Transfer

  • Pre-booked or available at the airport
  • Drop-off directly at your hotel
  • Travel time: ~30 minutes
  • Approximate amount: 15-25 USD

Taxi

  • Available directly outside the terminal
  • Travel time: ~25-30 minutes
  • Safe and widely used
  • Estimated cost: 20-30 USD

Bus / Shuttle

  • Budget-friendly option
  • Travel time: ~30-40 minutes depending on route
  • Estimated cost: 2 USD
  • Departures from Puerto Montt bus terminal

Taxis and transfers are the most convenient options for international travelers.

Final destination

Puerto Varas

Puerto Varas is a scenic lakeside city located in southern Chile, known for Lake Llanquihue, Osorno Volcano, and easy access to national parks and research sites.

Travel Tips

  • Currency: Chilean Peso (CLP)
  • Most taxis and services accept cards, but carrying some cash is recommended
  • Weather can change quickly - bring appropriate clothing
  • Spanish is the local language, but basic English is commonly understood in tourism services
